AI Automated PowerPoint Reporting — FAQ

AI automated PowerPoint reporting is the process of using artificial intelligence to generate corporate PowerPoint slide decks from your data automatically — no manual formatting, no copy-pasting, no chart-updating. Octigen connects to your data sources (Excel, databases, APIs), learns your existing PowerPoint templates, and produces fully-editable, pixel-perfect reports every time you run them.

In four steps: upload your existing PowerPoint template, connect your data sources, define a reusable workflow specifying which slides to generate and in what order, and run it. Octigen's AI generates the complete, branded report automatically — and you can re-run it every month, quarter, or week without rebuilding anything.

Any recurring corporate report that follows a consistent template and uses live data: fund factsheets and quarterly investor reports, client-facing business presentations, sales pitch decks from CRM data, monthly KPI decks, and more. If you rebuild it every period from the same template, Octigen can automate it.
