Our Story

Built by practitioners,
for practitioners

We've spent years in finance and technology—building data systems, running analyses, and creating the reports that drive decisions. Octigen is born from that experience.

The tools on the market were either too complex or couldn't produce native PowerPoint. So we built our own.

At Fisch Asset Management, Michel faced a common problem: existing reporting tools were too complex to use and couldn't export to PowerPoint properly. So he built a state-of-the-art reporting system from scratch.

When Kevin joined, he helped push the system further—streamlining workflows and expanding its capabilities. Together, they saw how much value a purpose-built solution could deliver.

That's when the idea for Octigen emerged: a more generic platform that could work for any company, with AI integrated from the ground up.
